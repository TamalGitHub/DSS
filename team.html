Mr. Chandan Ghosh

FCA, AICMA, LL.B, FAFD, FXTM, IFRS, B.Com (H), Cert. Valuer, Cert.International Taxation

Chandan is in full time practice for the last 21 years. Apart from being a Senior Fellow member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) he is also a Fellow of the Institute of Cost & Works Accountants of India. He is the examiner of the both the Institutes. He has successfully completed the courses on ‘IFRS (International Financial Reporting Standards)’, ‘VALUATION’, ‘INTERNATIONAL TAXATION’, FOREX & TREASURY MANAGEMENT, FORENSIC ACCOUNTING & FRAUD DETECTION, CONCURRENT AUDIT OF BANKS conducted by the ICAI. He is empanelled as a Receiver of the High Court at Kolkata and is also empanelled as auditor in the office of the Liquidator of Companies apart from various other empanelments.

Mr Atindra N Sen

HR professional

Dr. Malabika Desai

Dr Desai, a Doctorate in Psychology, began her career as a business and HR professional, evolved as a Coach and a Facilitator, and later took a break as a full time academics.

Dr Desai served as Principal of RCG Management Consulting and has been a visiting faculty with some eminent Indian business schools, viz., IIM Calcutta (IIMC), Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT), Chartered Financial Institute (ICFAI), and also been an invited speaker at many International and National conventions. Dr Desai served as a full time faculty for Organisational Behaviour and Human Resource Management in the Indian Institute of Management Shillong (IIMS).
